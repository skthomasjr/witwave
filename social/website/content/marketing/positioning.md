# Positioning notes

## Core thesis

Agentic AI does not transform software delivery just because teams use agents. Transformation starts when agents become
part of the development lifecycle: work routing, context, evals, quality gates, memory, observability, and human
escalation.

## Public framing

witwave is about agent-native engineering: teams of governed agents that can work with durable identity, memory,
coordination, and safety boundaries.

## Avoid

- Do not claim humans are removed from software delivery.
- Do not imply all teams should become fully agent-native immediately.
- Do not sell autonomy without the control system around it.
- Do not frame cloud or Kubernetes runtime as equivalent to autonomy.
